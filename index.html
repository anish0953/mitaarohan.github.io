AAROHAN

The Cultural Fest

Aarohan is the annual cultural festival of MITWPU and the largest college fest of Pune.

About Us

Ever since its inception in 2014, Aarohan has shown exponential growth with a plethora of alluring events, performances, and activities. In a 3 day long fiesta, Aarohan embodies the knack and passion of young potentials from all over the country by giving them the perfect platform to showcase their talents. Aarohan has always cast a spell over the audience and has provided absolute entertainment through its competitive events and magnificent performances by renowned country artists of the country.

SOCIAL CAUSE

Tree Plantation

MIT-WPU Aarohan organized a tree plantation drive in 2019 under its campaign 'Plant the Future'. A total of 501 trees were planted.

MARATHON

MIT-WPU Aarohan has organized multiple iterations of Pune's largest Run for a Cause Marathon to generate funds for the education of the underprivileged.

Pride Month

MIT-WPU Aarohan expressed its support towards the LGBTQIA+ community through an Instagram Live in 2020 with an aim to spread awareness about the same.
